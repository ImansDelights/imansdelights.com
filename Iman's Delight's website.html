About our Business

Iman's Delight's is a fully halaal family bakery on 51 Grunter Str. West Ridge, Mosselbay. Preparing our Food is based on simple, unfussy processes and values, and we work with a small store of some seasonal ingredients. And we are passionate about making the best quality product and we take our time to guarantee this.

If you wish to support us, please make a little donation via PayPal. That would be very helpful. Another way is to tell your friends about Iman's Delight's website. Thank you.
